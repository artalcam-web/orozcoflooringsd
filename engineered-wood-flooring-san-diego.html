Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about engineered wood flooring San Diego.

What is engineered wood flooring?
Engineered wood has a real hardwood veneer on top bonded to multiple layers of plywood. It looks identical to solid hardwood but handles humidity and temperature changes better.
Can engineered wood be refinished?
Yes — depending on the veneer thickness, engineered wood can be sanded and refinished 1–3 times, extending its lifespan significantly.
Is engineered wood good for San Diego?
It's actually better than solid hardwood for San Diego's coastal climate. The layered construction resists the humidity swings that cause solid wood to expand and contract.
How long does installation take?
Typically 1–2 days for an average home. Nail-down and glue-down methods both available depending on your subfloor.
