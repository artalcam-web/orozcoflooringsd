Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your flooring project.

How much does hardwood flooring installation cost in San Diego?
Hardwood flooring installation in San Diego typically ranges from $5 to $15 per square foot depending on the species, width, and subfloor condition. We provide a detailed written estimate after a free in-home measurement — no surprises.
What's the difference between LVP and hardwood?
Luxury Vinyl Plank (LVP) is 100% waterproof, more scratch-resistant, and generally more affordable than hardwood. Hardwood offers a more premium, natural feel and can be refinished multiple times over its lifetime. Both are excellent choices — we help you decide based on your lifestyle and budget.
Do you remove old flooring before installing the new one?
Yes. We include old floor removal and disposal in our project quotes. Whether it's carpet, tile, or old laminate, we handle the demolition and haul-away so you don't have to worry about it.
How long does flooring installation take?
Most residential projects are completed in 1 to 3 days depending on the square footage and type of flooring. We give you a realistic timeline upfront and stick to it.
Are you licensed and insured in California?
Yes. Orozco Flooring is a fully licensed California flooring contractor, fully insured for liability and workers' compensation. Your home and your family are protected throughout the project.
Do you offer financing or payment plans?
We accept cash, check, and major credit cards. Contact us to ask about flexible payment arrangements for larger projects — we're happy to work with you.
