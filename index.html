Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your flooring project.

How much does LVP flooring installation cost in San Diego?
Cost depends on square footage, subfloor condition, and the material you choose. Orozco Flooring provides a free, detailed written estimate after an in-home measurement — no surprises, no commitment. Call or text (619) 761-4435.
What's the difference between LVP and engineered wood?
Luxury Vinyl Plank (LVP) is 100% waterproof and highly scratch-resistant — great for kitchens, bathrooms, and homes with pets or kids. Engineered wood has a real hardwood top layer, offering a more premium, natural look and feel. Both are excellent options; we help you choose based on your lifestyle.
Do you remove old flooring before installing new?
Yes. Old floor removal and disposal is included in our quotes. Whether it's old tile or laminate, our team handles the demo so you don't have to worry about it.
Can you repair water or flood-damaged floors?
Absolutely. Orozco Flooring specializes in flood repair, subfloor reinforcements, and full floor replacement after water damage. We also handle squeak repairs and scratch/dent restoration.
What flooring brands do you use?
We source from trusted brands including Urban Surfaces, Paradigm, and Republic — known for quality, durability, and warranty coverage. We'll help you pick the right product for your budget and lifestyle.
Do you serve areas outside of San Diego?
Yes. We serve all of Southern California, including Los Angeles, Orange County, Riverside, and the wider SoCal region. Contact us to confirm availability in your area.
