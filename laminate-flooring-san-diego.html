Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about laminate flooring San Diego.

What is laminate flooring?
Laminate is a multi-layer synthetic floor with a photographic layer that mimics wood or stone, topped with a clear protective coating. It's fast to install and very affordable.
Is laminate or LVP better?
LVP is 100% waterproof and more durable underfoot. Laminate is slightly less expensive and faster to install — great for bedrooms, living rooms, and dry areas.
How long does laminate installation take?
Laminate is one of the fastest floors to install. Most rooms are done in a single day.
Can laminate be installed over existing floors?
Often yes, depending on height clearance and subfloor condition. We assess during your free in-home estimate.
