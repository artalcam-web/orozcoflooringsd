Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about LVP flooring San Diego.

What is LVP flooring?
Luxury Vinyl Plank (LVP) is a multi-layer synthetic flooring that looks like real wood but is 100% waterproof and much more durable. It's the most popular flooring choice in San Diego homes.
How much does LVP installation cost in San Diego?
LVP installation in San Diego typically ranges from $3–$7 per sq ft installed. Materials start at $1.99/sq ft. Orozco Flooring provides free in-home estimates with no commitment.
How long does LVP installation take?
Most rooms are completed in 1 day. A full house (1,000–2,000 sq ft) typically takes 2–3 days depending on subfloor preparation.
Is LVP good for San Diego's climate?
Yes. LVP handles San Diego's temperature swings and coastal humidity better than hardwood. It won't warp, swell, or crack.
